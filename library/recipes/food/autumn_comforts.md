# Butternut Squash Soup

Season: autumn | Vibe: cozy, comforting, smooth | Meal type: lunch, dinner | Difficulty: easy | Time: 45 min
Ingredients: butternut squash, onion, garlic, vegetable stock, olive oil, sage, black pepper, cream (optional)
Instructions: 1. Roast or sauté the squash, onion, and garlic until softened. 2. Add stock and simmer until the squash is fully tender. 3. Blend until smooth. 4. Finish with sage, black pepper, and a small swirl of cream if you want it richer.

# Roasted Tomato Soup

Season: autumn | Vibe: cozy, savory, simple | Meal type: lunch, dinner | Difficulty: easy | Time: 45 min
Ingredients: tomatoes, onion, garlic, olive oil, stock, basil, butter, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Roast tomatoes, onion, and garlic until deeply soft and lightly caramelized. 2. Transfer to a pot with stock and simmer briefly. 3. Blend until smooth. 4. Stir in butter or a splash of cream if you want a softer finish.

# Chicken and Rice Soup

Season: autumn, winter | Vibe: cozy, gentle, nourishing | Meal type: lunch, dinner | Difficulty: easy | Time: 45 min
Ingredients: chicken thighs or cooked chicken, rice, carrot, celery, onion, garlic, chicken stock, parsley, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery until softened. 2. Add stock and rice and simmer until the rice is tender. 3. Stir in shredded chicken and warm through. 4. Finish with parsley, salt, and black pepper.

# Pumpkin Soup with Croutons

Season: autumn | Vibe: cozy, seasonal, warm | Meal type: lunch, dinner | Difficulty: easy | Time: 40 min
Ingredients: pumpkin puree or roasted pumpkin, onion, garlic, stock, cream, nutmeg, olive oil, bread, butter, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Cook onion and garlic until soft, then add pumpkin and stock. 2. Simmer until smooth and cohesive, then blend if needed. 3. Stir in a little cream and nutmeg. 4. Toast bread cubes in butter for croutons and serve on top.

# Pumpkin Cinnamon Bread

Season: autumn | Vibe: cozy, fun, sweet | Meal type: breakfast, snack | Difficulty: easy | Time: 60 min
Ingredients: pumpkin puree, flour, sugar, eggs, butter or oil, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking powder, salt
Instructions: 1. Mix the wet ingredients in one bowl and the dry ingredients in another. 2. Combine just until the batter comes together. 3. Bake in a loaf pan until the center is set and a tester comes out clean. 4. Cool before slicing.

# Apple Cinnamon Loaf

Season: autumn | Vibe: cozy, sweet, homey | Meal type: breakfast, snack, dessert | Difficulty: easy | Time: 65 min
Ingredients: apples, flour, sugar, eggs, butter, cinnamon, brown sugar, baking powder, salt, vanilla
Instructions: 1. Dice or thinly slice the apples. 2. Mix a simple loaf batter with cinnamon and vanilla. 3. Fold in the apples and bake until golden and set. 4. Let cool before slicing so the crumb holds together.

# Maple Oatmeal with Stewed Apples

Season: autumn, winter | Vibe: cozy, simple, breakfasty | Meal type: breakfast | Difficulty: easy | Time: 20 min
Ingredients: rolled oats, milk or water, apples, maple syrup, cinnamon, butter, pinch of salt
Instructions: 1. Simmer the oats with milk or water and a pinch of salt until creamy. 2. In a small pan, cook chopped apples with butter, cinnamon, and maple syrup until soft. 3. Spoon the apples over the oatmeal. 4. Serve warm.

# Baked Potato with Butter and Chives

Season: autumn, winter | Vibe: simple, cozy, plain | Meal type: lunch, dinner, snack | Difficulty: easy | Time: 60 min
Ingredients: russet potatoes, butter, chives, flaky salt, black pepper, sour cream (optional)
Instructions: 1. Bake the potatoes until crisp outside and fluffy inside. 2. Split them open and fluff the insides with a fork. 3. Add butter, salt, pepper, and chives. 4. Add a spoon of sour cream if you want it richer.

# Creamy Mushroom Toast

Season: autumn | Vibe: cozy, savory, cafe-style | Meal type: lunch, snack | Difficulty: easy | Time: 20 min
Ingredients: mushrooms, butter, garlic, thyme, cream, bread, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Sauté the mushrooms in butter until browned and their moisture cooks off. 2. Add garlic and thyme, then stir in a small splash of cream. 3. Toast the bread until crisp. 4. Spoon the mushrooms over the toast and finish with black pepper.
