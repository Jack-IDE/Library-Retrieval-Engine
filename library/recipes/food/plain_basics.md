# Simple Scrambled Eggs and Buttered Toast

Season: all-season | Vibe: plain, simple, quick | Meal type: breakfast | Difficulty: easy | Time: 10 min
Ingredients: 2 eggs, 2 slices bread, butter, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Toast the bread until golden. 2. Melt butter in a pan over low to medium heat. 3. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt, then cook slowly, stirring until softly scrambled. 4. Butter the toast and serve the eggs on the side or on top.

# Fried Eggs and Toast

Season: all-season | Vibe: plain, quick, classic | Meal type: breakfast | Difficulty: easy | Time: 10 min
Ingredients: 2 eggs, 2 slices bread, butter or oil, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Toast the bread until crisp and warm. 2. Heat a little butter or oil in a skillet. 3. Fry the eggs until the whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your liking. 4. Season with salt and pepper and serve with buttered toast.

# Soft-Boiled Eggs and Soldiers

Season: all-season | Vibe: simple, playful, cozy | Meal type: breakfast, snack | Difficulty: easy | Time: 12 min
Ingredients: 2 eggs, 2 slices bread, butter, flaky salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Bring water to a gentle boil and lower in the eggs. 2. Cook for 6 to 7 minutes for jammy centers. 3. Toast the bread, butter it, and cut it into strips. 4. Crack the eggs into cups, season, and dip the toast strips into the yolk.

# Cinnamon Toast

Season: autumn, winter | Vibe: simple, cozy, sweet | Meal type: breakfast, snack | Difficulty: easy | Time: 10 min
Ingredients: 2 slices bread, softened butter, sugar, cinnamon, pinch of salt
Instructions: 1. Stir together sugar, cinnamon, and a tiny pinch of salt. 2. Spread butter on the bread and sprinkle the cinnamon sugar evenly over the top. 3. Toast or bake until the edges are crisp and the topping is bubbling. 4. Let cool briefly before slicing.

# Basic Tomato Soup

Season: autumn, winter | Vibe: cozy, simple, classic | Meal type: lunch, dinner | Difficulty: easy | Time: 35 min
Ingredients: canned tomatoes, onion, garlic, olive oil, vegetable stock, butter, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil and a little butter until soft. 2. Add tomatoes and stock, then simmer for 20 minutes. 3. Blend until smooth. 4. Taste and adjust with salt and pepper before serving.

# Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup

Season: autumn, winter | Vibe: cozy, comforting, classic | Meal type: lunch, dinner | Difficulty: easy | Time: 30 min
Ingredients: sandwich bread, cheddar, butter, canned tomatoes, onion, garlic, stock, salt, black pepper
Instructions: 1. Start a basic tomato soup by simmering tomatoes, onion, garlic, and stock. 2. Butter the bread, fill with cheddar, and griddle until crisp outside and melted inside. 3. Blend the soup until smooth. 4. Serve the grilled cheese alongside the hot soup for dipping.
