# Seasonal Comfort Anchors

Use these anchors when the query is broad, mood-based, or seasonal.

Autumn / cozy soup anchors:
- Butternut Squash Soup
- Roasted Tomato Soup
- Chicken and Rice Soup
- Pumpkin Soup with Croutons

Plain / simple breakfast anchors:
- Simple Scrambled Eggs and Buttered Toast
- Fried Eggs and Toast
- Soft-Boiled Eggs and Soldiers
- Cinnamon Toast

Autumn bread and snack anchors:
- Pumpkin Cinnamon Bread
- Apple Cinnamon Loaf
- Maple Oatmeal with Stewed Apples
- Creamy Mushroom Toast
- Baked Potato with Butter and Chives
- Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup
