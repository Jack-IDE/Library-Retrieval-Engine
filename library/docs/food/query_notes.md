# Food Query Notes

Use this section for recipe and meal queries.

Good fits:
- direct recipe lookups
- ingredient-based questions
- cuisine/category browsing
- recommendation prompts grounded in recipe content
- seasonal prompts like autumn soup, cozy dinner, winter breakfast
- broad mood prompts like simple breakfast, comfort food, fun bread

Useful retrieval anchors:
- season tags: autumn, winter, spring, summer, all-season
- vibe tags: cozy, comforting, simple, plain, quick, fun, sweet, savory
- meal tags: breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, dessert
- difficulty tags: easy, medium, advanced

Preferred plain/cozy anchors:
- Simple Scrambled Eggs and Buttered Toast
- Fried Eggs and Toast
- Basic Tomato Soup
- Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup
- Butternut Squash Soup
- Pumpkin Cinnamon Bread
- Apple Cinnamon Loaf

Less ideal:
- programming/tooling questions
- architecture/spec questions
- library-maintenance notes mixed into user-facing food content
